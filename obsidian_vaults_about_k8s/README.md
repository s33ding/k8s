
# Obsidian Vault

Welcome to this Obsidian vault repository! This repository contains a vault for Obsidian, a powerful knowledge base that works on top of a local folder of plain text Markdown files.

## What is Obsidian?

Obsidian is a versatile application that helps you connect your thoughts and organize your notes with links, making it ideal for managing complex systems of knowledge. Itâ€™s especially popular among researchers, students, writers, and anyone juggling lots of information.

### Key Features

- **Linking Thoughts**: Easily link thoughts together to create a web of knowledge.
- **Markdown Support**: All notes are stored in Markdown format.
- **Customizable**: Extensive plugin and theming support to tailor the experience to your needs.
- **Offline First**: All your notes are locally stored on your machine, ensuring privacy and control over your data.

## How to Download and Install Obsidian

1. **Visit the Official Website**: Go to [Obsidian's official website](https://obsidian.md).
2. **Download the App**: Select the version of the app suitable for your operating system (Windows, macOS, Linux).
3. **Install the Application**: Follow the installation prompts to install Obsidian on your computer.
